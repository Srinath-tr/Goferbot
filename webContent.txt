<h2 id="mp-tfa-h2" style="margin:3px; background:#cef2e0; font-family:inherit; font-size:120%; font-weight:bold; border:1px solid #a3bfb1; text-align:left; color:#000; padding:0.2em 0.4em;"><span class="mw-headline" id="From_today.27s_featured_article">From today's featured article</span></h2>

<td style="color:#000;">
<div id="mp-tfa" style="padding:2px 5px">
<div id="mp-tfa-img" style="float: left; margin: 0.5em 0.9em 0.4em 0em;">
<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 152px;"><a href="/wiki/File:USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg" class="image" title="Scene depicting the action of 9 February 1799"><img alt="Scene depicting the action of 9 February 1799" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/ff/USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg/152px-USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg" width="152" height="120" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/ff/USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg/228px-USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/ff/USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg/304px-USSConstellationVsInsurgente.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4996" data-file-height="3946" /></a></div>
</div>
<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/USS_Constellation_vs_L%27Insurgente" title="USS Constellation vs L'Insurgente">USS <i>Constellation</i> captured the French <i>L'Insurgente</i></a></b> in a <a href="/wiki/Single-ship_action" class="mw-redirect" title="Single-ship action">single-ship action</a> between the <a href="/wiki/Frigate" title="Frigate">frigates</a> on 9 February 1799. The previous year, French <a href="/wiki/Privateer" title="Privateer">privateering</a> attacks against American vessels had led to the <a href="/wiki/Undeclared_war" title="Undeclared war">undeclared</a> <a href="/wiki/Quasi-War" title="Quasi-War">Quasi-War</a>. Four US naval <a href="/wiki/Squadron_(naval)" title="Squadron (naval)">squadrons</a> were sent to the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a> with orders to seize armed French vessels and prevent attacks on American ships. The squadron under Commodore <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Truxtun" title="Thomas Truxtun">Thomas Truxtun</a> was on assignment in the waters between <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a> and <a href="/wiki/Saint_Kitts" title="Saint Kitts">Saint Kitts</a> when his <a href="/wiki/Flagship" title="Flagship">flagship</a> <i>Constellation</i>, cruising independently, met and engaged <i>L'Insurgente</i>, commanded by Michel-Pierre Barreaut. After chasing the French ship through a storm, Truxtun forced an engagement. The French frigate surrendered after 74 minutes with heavy casualties; the Americans sustained only a few casualties. <i>L'Insurgente</i> was taken to Saint Kitts and commissioned into the United States Navy as <a href="/wiki/USS_Insurgent_(1799)" class="mw-redirect" title="USS Insurgent (1799)">USS&#160;<i>Insurgent</i></a>. The action was the first victory over an enemy warship for the newly formed navy, and Truxtun was praised by the American government and public. (<a href="/wiki/USS_Constellation_vs_L%27Insurgente" title="USS Constellation vs L'Insurgente"><b>Full&#160;article...</b></a>)</p>

<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 100px;"><a href="/wiki/File:John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_(detail),_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG" class="image" title="John Quincy Adams"><img alt="John Quincy Adams" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/37/John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_%28detail%29%2C_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG/100px-John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_%28detail%29%2C_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG" width="100" height="133" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/37/John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_%28detail%29%2C_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG/150px-John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_%28detail%29%2C_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/37/John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_%28detail%29%2C_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG/200px-John_Quincy_Adams_by_George_Caleb_Bingham_%28detail%29%2C_c._1850_after_1844_original_-_DSC03235.JPG 2x" data-file-width="3240" data-file-height="4320" /></a>
<div class="thumbcaption" style="padding: 0.25em 0; word-wrap: break-word;">John Quincy Adams</div>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1825" title="1825">1825</a> – After no presidential candidate received a majority of <a href="/wiki/Electoral_College_(United_States)" title="Electoral College (United States)">electoral votes</a>, the United States House of Representatives <a href="/wiki/United_States_presidential_election,_1824" title="United States presidential election, 1824">elected</a> <b><a href="/wiki/John_Quincy_Adams" title="John Quincy Adams">John Quincy Adams</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> president.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Svalbard_Treaty" title="Svalbard Treaty">Svalbard Treaty</a> was signed, recognising Norwegian sovereignty over the Arctic archipelago of <b><a href="/wiki/Svalbard" title="Svalbard">Svalbard</a></b>, but all signatories were also given equal rights to engage in commercial activities on the islands.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: <a href="/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_II" title="Allies of World War II">Allied</a> forces declared <a href="/wiki/Guadalcanal" title="Guadalcanal">Guadalcanal</a> secure, ending the <b><a href="/wiki/Guadalcanal_Campaign" title="Guadalcanal Campaign">Guadalcanal Campaign</a></b> as a significant strategic victory for Allied forces fighting Japan in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_War" title="Pacific War">Pacific War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1969" title="1969">1969</a> – The <b><a href="/wiki/Boeing_747" title="Boeing 747">Boeing 747</a></b> made its first flight, with test pilots Jack Waddell and Brien Wygle at the controls and Jess Wallick at the flight engineer's station.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> – Breaking a seventeen-month ceasefire, the <a href="/wiki/Provisional_Irish_Republican_Army" title="Provisional Irish Republican Army">Provisional Irish Republican Army</a> <b><a href="/wiki/1996_Docklands_bombing" title="1996 Docklands bombing">detonated a powerful truck bomb</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Canary_Wharf" title="Canary Wharf">Canary Wharf</a>, London, killing 2 people and injuring more than 100 others.</li>
</ul>

<td style="padding:0 6px 0 0">
<p><b><a href="/wiki/George_W._Campbell" title="George W. Campbell">George W. Campbell</a></b> (1769–1848) was an American statesman who served as a <a href="/wiki/United_States_House_of_Representatives" title="United States House of Representatives">U.S. Representative</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States_Senator" class="mw-redirect" title="United States Senator">Senator</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tennessee_Supreme_Court" title="Tennessee Supreme Court">Tennessee Supreme Court Justice</a>, and <a href="/wiki/United_States_Ambassador_to_Russia" title="United States Ambassador to Russia">U.S. Ambassador to Russia</a>. Appointed fifth <a href="/wiki/United_States_Secretary_of_the_Treasury" title="United States Secretary of the Treasury">United States Secretary of the Treasury</a> by <a href="/wiki/James_Madison" title="James Madison">James Madison</a>, he faced national financial disorder brought on by the <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>. Campbell was unsuccessful in his efforts to raise enough money through <a href="/wiki/Government_bond" title="Government bond">government bond</a> sales and he resigned after only eight months in office, disillusioned and in bad health.</p>
